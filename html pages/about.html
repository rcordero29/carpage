Where We Started

What began as merely a hobby in his household garage grew exponentially from a part-time trade to a lifelong pursuit. Since his first high performance car, a Nissan 350Z to a Lexus IS-F, he has crafted his passion into every facet of the vehicle, from car performance to flawless paint finishing. His collection expanded to include a rare Nismo S-Tune R34 Nissan Skyline GT-R , 2013 Nissan GT-R. Improving upon perfection became his aim and mission, not only for his own cars, but all cars.


Next Generation Equipment

As an innovative car care studio, We take detailing and car care to the next level by utilizing the finest paint assessment digital gauges, infrared thermal curing lamps, digital microscope system, pneumatic powered tools, paint refinement machines, automotive rendered lighting, controlled clean room as well as eco-friendly and VOC-compliant chemicals. No imperfection goes undetected and this ensures that our work is flawless prior to delivery.
